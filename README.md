# BuildBetter — Field Notes for Technical Founders

> GTM strategy, B2B sales, and growth thinking — written by someone who's been in the trenches across SaaS, open-source, software, hardware, and enterprise for 20+ years.

This repo is a public archive of the **[BuildBetter Newsletter]([https://YOUR-NEWSLETTER-URL-HERE](https://buildbetterhq.substack.com/))** and previous (now retired) blogs — articles on how technical founders can stop building in a vacuum and actually get their product to market and turning them from academic exercises into businesses.

---

## What You'll Find Here

Articles covering the stuff technical founders usually figure out too late:

- **Go-to-market strategy** — positioning, ICP definition, messaging that doesn't sound like every other SaaS
- **B2B sales** — how to sell without feeling like a salesperson
- **Partnerships & distribution** — finding leverage beyond paid ads
- **Founder mindset** — the mental models behind good business founder decisions

No fluff. No "10x your growth" hot takes (unfortunately). Just thinking out loud from the trenches.
